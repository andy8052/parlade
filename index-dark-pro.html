What is Parlade?

Parlade is a platform where users can buy, sell and trade bets on the blockchain.

Existing platforms currently are transparent, sketchy or outdated. We have created a system which sends users funds to a smart contract which they controll. Funds can be withdrawn at any time.

Users can also trade their live bets on a decentralized exchange with other bettors. This allows for a more robust system where users truly OWN their bets.
